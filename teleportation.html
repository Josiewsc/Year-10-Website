TELEPORTATION

You got teleportation! You are everywhere yet nowhere. You're either very early or fashionably late to functions but never on time. You always are visiting cool, new places that are to die for. You can't stand still, you just love going around and doing stuff. Whether it's art, sports, travelling or shopping you're always on the move. You treat those who are close to you with deep care and love and they return the favour by expressing their care and love to you consistently. Those who hangout with you never have dull moments. When you are around your smile truly lights up a whole city.
